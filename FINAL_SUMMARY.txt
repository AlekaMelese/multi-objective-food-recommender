RESPONSIBLE AI ANALYSIS (NEW - ENHANCED)
================================================================================

✅ Risks of Nudging Behavior:
• Over-Restriction (Low α): Paternalistic, reduces engagement
• Freedom of Choice (High α): Enables unhealthy patterns
• Balance Question: Who decides the "right" α value?

✅ Personalization Fairness Across Dietary Groups:
• Cultural Preferences: WHO scores may penalize non-Western cuisines
• Medical Conditions: One-size-fits-all fails for diabetics, allergies
• Socioeconomic: Health optimization may favor privileged users

✅ Recommendations:
• User-controllable α parameter
• Complete opt-out option
• Personalized health profiles
• Transparent explanations
• Fairness monitoring across demographics
